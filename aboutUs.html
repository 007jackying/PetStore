About Pet Store & Vet Clinic

It's been 15 years since We have worked veterinary services. We have founded this center since 2006. Importantly, the basic principles of our operation have stayed the same to this day.

We always put our 5 principles above everything else and we apply them in our daily practices:

  1. Focusing on our staff experience, rather than its size
  2. Using the best, most advanced medical equipment
  3. Providing an affordable price tag to all of our services
  4. Keeping close touch with our colleagues for the Second Opinion
  5. Covering wide range of services as possible
